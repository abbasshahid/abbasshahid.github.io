About Me

I belong to Mianwali, Pakistan and currently living in Vienna Austria. I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science from COMSATS University Islamabad, Pakistan where I specialized in Blockchain technology, focusing on privacy-preserving authentication and malicious node detection for optimized IoT network routing. My academic journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Telecommunication and networking from the same esteemed institution. Professionally, I have gain significant experience as a Blockchain Researcher at the Austrian Blockchain Center in Vienna, Austria, where I contributed to projects involving Polygon and Hyperledger Fabric Blockchain technologies, and evaluated IoT-based blockchain clients. Prior to this, I served as a Research Associate at the ComSense Lab, Department of Computer Science, COMSATS University Islamabad. My research work has led to few publications in prestigious journals, and I have submitted/published at notable conferences including the ACM/SIGAPP Symposium on Applied Computing. My research interests include Blockchain, the Internet of Things, Privacy, and trust-related issues.
